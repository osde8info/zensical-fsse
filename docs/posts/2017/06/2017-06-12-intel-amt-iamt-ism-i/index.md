Intel AMT IAMT ISBT vulnerability (INTEL-SA-00075) may affect business PCs such as DELL Optiplexs and HP

- https://security-center.intel.com/advisories.aspx
- https://security-center.intel.com/advisory.aspx?intelid=INTEL-SA-00075&languageid=en-fr
- https://downloadcenter.intel.com/download/26755

DELL

- http://www.dell.com/support/article/uk/en/ukbsdt1/SLN306228/dell-client-statement-on-intel-amt-advisory--intel-sa-00075-?lang=EN

_Dell is aware of an industry-wide vulnerability described in this security bulletin from Intel that can affect Dell PCs that support system manageability via Intel Active Management Technology (AMT), Intel Small Business Technology (SBT), or Intel Standard Manageability (ISM). We are actively developing BIOS firmware updates to help customers mitigate their vulnerability._

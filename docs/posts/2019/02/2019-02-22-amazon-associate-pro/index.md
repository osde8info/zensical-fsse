_Amazon Associates members participating in an Amazon Associate Program in the US, UK, DE, FR, IT, ES, or CA, with a bank account in the US (USD) the United Kingdom (GBP and EUR) or Eurozone (EUR) – a total of 52 countries – can now receive their international payments by bank transfer in their local currency to their bank account._

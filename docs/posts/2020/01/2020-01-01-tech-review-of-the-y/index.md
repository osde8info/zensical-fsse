[Stack Overflow](https://insights.stackoverflow.com/survey/2019) technology review of the year 2019 (2019-2020) _each month, about 50 million people visit Stack Overflow_

- [IDEs](https://insights.stackoverflow.com/survey/2019#development-environments-and-tools) - top 3 are VSCode, VS, Notepad++
- [langs](https://insights.stackoverflow.com/survey/2019#technology) - top 4 are JS/TS, HTML/CSS, SQL, Python

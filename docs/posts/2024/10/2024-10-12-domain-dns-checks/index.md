Check your Domain DNS setting with [Google Apps Toolbox](https://toolbox.googleapps.com/apps/)

You may get some errors like these

MAIL MX DNS checks:

_error_

Domain must have at least one mail server.

[Help center article](https://support.google.com/a/answer/174125)

_warning_

DKIM is not set up.

[Help center article](https://support.google.com/a/answer/174124)

_warning_

DMARC is not set up.

[Help center article](https://support.google.com/a/answer/2466580)

_warning_

MTA-STS DNS Record.

[Help center article](https://support.google.com/a/answer/2466580)

_warning_

There should be a valid SPF record.

[Help center article](https://support.google.com/a/answer/33786)

_warning_

No Google mail exchangers found. Relayhost configuration?

[Help center article](https://support.google.com/a/answer/33915)

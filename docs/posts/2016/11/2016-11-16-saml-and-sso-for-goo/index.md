#saml and #sso for #goog & @box

https://support.google.com/a/answer/6356730?hl=en

_You must be signed in as a super administrator for this task._

_Using Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML), your users can use their Google Cloud credentials to sign in to enterprise-cloud applications._

_As administrator, you have to configure a few things to make it work, including:_

- _Set up the selected application as a SAML service provider (SP)._
- _Set up G Suite as a SAML identity provider (IdP)._
- _Enter application-specific service provider details in Google Admin console._
- _Turn on single sign-on (SSO) for the application._
- _Verify that the SSO is working._
